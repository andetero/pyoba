Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE: MARCH 2026

GIST is a daily word puzzle game. This policy explains what information is collected when you play and how it is used.

WHAT WE COLLECT

When you complete a puzzle, GIST records the following anonymously: the puzzle number, the number of guesses used, and whether the puzzle was solved. No personal information is collected — no name, email address, location, or device identifier.

HOW IT'S USED

This anonymous score data is used solely to calculate community statistics shown in the app — total players, solve rate, and average guesses. It is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

DATA STORAGE

Anonymous scores are stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database service. Your puzzle progress is stored locally on your device and never transmitted.

THIRD PARTY SERVICES

GIST uses Supabase for anonymous score storage. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

CHILDREN

GIST does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

CHANGES

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
